Our Vulnerability

  • Modern banking relies on a complex ecosystem of 3rd-party providers (payments, trading & settlement, data, etc.)
  • An incident at a 3rd-party can directly impact our services, customer trust, and revenue
  • A single point of failure is an unacceptable risk

Our Objective

  • Transform from reactive to proactive resilience

Circuit Breakers

Automatically "trip" to stop sending requests to a failing service. Protects our systems from cascading failures.

üìâ

Graceful Service Degradation

Instead of a hard error, offer a limited but functional experience. Example: "CPFB is down, we will process your SGS application manually."

üì±

Proactive Customer Communication

Use in-app banners, SMS, and email to inform customers immediately. Reduces anxiety and inbound calls.
